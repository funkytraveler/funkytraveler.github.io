Introducing your workspace

What is a workspace?

A workspace is trading environment tailored to your preferences. It contains a collection of widgets you use to monitor prices, place orders, track fills, and more. You can use create a single workspace for all of your trading, or you can create multiple workspaces with widgets suited to specific trading needs. For example, you can:

  • Create separate workspaces for different markets, brokers, or customers
  • Reflect your changing trading environment
  • Create a workspace that has a preformatted Fill window that shows specific information
