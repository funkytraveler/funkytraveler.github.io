Creating Your First Workspace

Creating a workspace

Creating a workspace is easy. Simply click Create a workspace and give it a name.

And your new workspace opens.

Changing the look of your workspace

If you don’t like the workspace background, you can change it.

  1. From the Settings menu, select Change background.

    (You might be wondering about that Change theme item. We’ll look at that one when we talk about launching widgets.)

  2. Browse through the different backgrounds until you find one you like.

    As you select different choices, the background dynamically updates.

  3. When you find the one you like, click Close.
