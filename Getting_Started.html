Before You Begin

Create Your User Account

The first step to access our new cloud-based trading environment is to create a user account. Simply access our home page, click the register button and enter your name, email address, and create a password. You will receive a notice in your email that your account is now active.

Joining a Company

The next step involves linking your account to the Clearing Firm. Login to your account and select the Settings gear icon. Select Invitations to view and accept any pending invites from your Clearing Firm. Once accepted, your account is fully activated and you can immediately begin to trade.

Note: Issues with accessing market data may indicate that the firm needs to complete your risk setup. Please contact your Clearing Firm if you experience issues on your first login attempt.

Take the Tour

The software includes a Take a Tour workspace. Once opened, the tour will give you an introduction to the various widgets and available functionality.
